College students often have very irregular schedules. This makes planning certain activities harder than necessary. At Filler! we believe that entertainment should not bring more headache than necessary.


Let's Get Started

Let's talk technical

But how does this work? In 3 simple steps. Once you are logged in, you can connect you Google calendar account, using secure OAuth authentication. Then, based on your preferred event types, our algorihtm will search the web for the upcoming events close to you, that fit in your schedule. Chose the one(s) you prefer and we'll add them directly to you calendar!
